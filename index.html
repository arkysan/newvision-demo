Export Information Center

Each footer item below opens a focused explanation for buyers, dealers, and fleet importers.

Vehicles

New Energy (EV/PHEV)

EV, PHEV, hybrid, range-extended, and smart vehicle sourcing from China, including BYD, Tesla China, Li Auto, Xiaomi, Leapmotor, Tank Hi4-T, and other fast-moving models.

  • Battery/range notes included in quotes when available.
  • Best for importers targeting fuel savings, city fleets, and premium EV demand.

Traditional Fuel

Gasoline and diesel vehicles for markets where fuel infrastructure, resale demand, or service support still favors combustion platforms.

  • Sedans, SUVs, pickups, commercial use vehicles.
  • Quote requests should include market, emission rules, and preferred model year.

Premium Brands

Premium China-market and international-brand vehicles supplied from available stock or requested sourcing, with emphasis on condition, trim, mileage, and export suitability.

  • BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Tesla, Tank, Maextro, and similar listings.
  • Inspection photos and current stock confirmation are recommended before deposit.

Motorcycles

Motorcycles, electric motorcycles, scooters, and tricycles for dealer or fleet buyers who need compact mobility products alongside cars.

  • Useful for last-mile delivery, urban transport, and resale channels.
  • Quote should specify engine/electric type, quantity, and destination port.

Engineering Vehicles

Commercial, construction, and special-use vehicle sourcing where the buyer needs specific payload, application, attachment, or compliance requirements.

  • Best handled as a custom quote with photos/spec sheets.
  • Include operating environment, destination country, and required documents.

Services

Pre-Export Inspection

Before shipping, buyers can request photo/video checks, VIN confirmation, mileage/condition review, and loading documentation.

  • Recommended for used vehicles and premium units.
  • Inspection scope should be agreed before final invoice.

Export Documentation

Documents are prepared around the buyer, vehicle, route, and destination requirements. Typical packs may include invoice, packing details, vehicle records, and export clearance documents.

  • Exact requirements depend on destination country.
  • Buyer should confirm import rules with local broker before purchase.

Ocean Freight

Freight can be quoted by destination port using available vessel routes, vehicle type, quantity, and loading method.

  • FOB and CIF estimates are available on request.
  • Final rate depends on current carrier schedules and port charges.

Customs Clearance

New Vision can support export-side clearance and document flow. Import-side clearance is handled by the buyer or their local broker.

  • Destination duties, taxes, and registration rules are buyer-side.
  • Use a licensed local broker before placing large orders.

After-Sales Support

After-sales support focuses on document follow-up, supplier coordination, parts sourcing support, and communication after shipment.

  • Warranty terms vary by vehicle, seller, and destination.
  • Confirm service expectations in writing before final purchase.
